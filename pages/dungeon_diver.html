Dungeon Divers

Fall 2024
Role: Lead Programmer
Tools: Godot, GDScript
Platform: PC

Dungeon Divers is meant to be the groundwork for a top down RPG where the player can fight a variety of different enemies with a variety of weapons. The end result ended up being more of a demo than a full game due to a number a reasons.

This project was made by a team of 5 students over a semester. I was the lead programmer of the game; I made the player controller, weapons, enemy AIs and pitfall system. I developed everything but the main menu.

  • Worked as Lead Programmer of project
  • Communicated with a team of 5
  • Used Godot to make a scalable project
